Fluid Simulation

Use the Leap Motion to control fluids on screen

BRIEF

What did I Create it?

After I was give the go ahead to demostrate the Leap Motion prototype I designed a fluid simulation application to demostrate at the Brighton Digital Festival. The application was designed to quickly demostrate the 10 finger tracking via a simple but beautiful Open CL liquid simulation.
